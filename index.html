Welcome

About Our Ministry

Dedicated to proclaiming God's truth and equipping believers for a life of faith

Welcome to Narrow Path Ministries, where we are committed to helping believers navigate the profound journey of faith with wisdom, understanding, and biblical truth. Founded by Dr. Sunny Ezhumattoor, our ministry has served as a source of spiritual guidance since 2010.

"Our mission is to guide seekers and believers alike onto the narrow path that leads to eternal life, through deep biblical teaching and practical application of God's Word."

Through our comprehensive programs including online biblical education, published theological resources, and digital teachings, we strive to equip disciples who will impact their communities for Christ. We believe in the transformative power of Scripture and its ability to change lives when properly understood and applied.

Join us as we explore the depths of God's Word together, growing in faith, wisdom, and spiritual maturity. Our commitment is to provide solid biblical teaching through personalized instruction, scholarly publications, and accessible online resources that equip you for every good work.

Whether through Dr. Ezhumattoor's extensive collection of theological books or structured online biblical courses, Narrow Path Ministries offers multiple pathways for spiritual growth and biblical understanding. We invite you to discover how sound biblical teaching can transform your walk with Christ.
