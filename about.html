01

Academic Excellence

A foundation built on rigorous scholarship and diverse disciplines

Dr. Ezhumattoor's educational journey reflects a commitment to comprehensive understanding. Holding post-graduate degrees in Economics and History from Mar Thoma College, along with a Bachelor of Laws degree, his academic foundation bridges secular scholarship with theological wisdom.

His extensive knowledge encompasses economics, sociology, political science, philosophy, scientific humanism, and jurisprudence. This remarkable breadth of learning enables him to address contemporary Christian challenges through multiple intellectual lenses, offering insights that are both spiritually profound and academically rigorous.
