Thekkel Home Bible College

Comprehensive Theological Education for the Twenty-First Century

THEKKEL
Rightly Dividing the Word of Truth

The School of Christian Thought at Thekkel Home Bible College exists to serve and equip the Church for the twenty-first century. We provide students with exceptional interpretational skills in Old and New Testament studies, alongside fundamental knowledge in philosophy, history, sociology, psychology, world missions, theology, economics, world religions, and apologetics.

Our programs challenge and prepare students to display sound reasoning, communicate clearly, and demonstrate effective research skills. The goal of all programs is to foster both intellectual and spiritual growth as students and faculty alike seek to glorify God in all things.

Academic Programs

Areas of Study

Comprehensive biblical and theological education rooted in dispensational theology

01

Old Testament Studies

Comprehensive exploration of the Hebrew Scriptures with emphasis on interpretational skills, historical context, and prophetic literature. Students develop proficiency in understanding the continuity of God's redemptive plan.

02

New Testament Studies

In-depth analysis of the New Testament writings with focus on apostolic teachings, Greek text interpretation, and the establishment of the early church. Special emphasis on Pauline epistles and prophetic books.

03

Systematic Theology

Dispensational theology with special emphasis on the distinctives of the Plymouth Brethren Movement. Students examine major doctrines through a biblical framework, understanding God's progressive revelation.
